The Post Office is open Monday to Saturday, 09:30am to 12:30pm.

The Shop is open 7 days a week from 8:30am to 2:00pm.

The deli-café is open 7 days a week from 8:30am to 1:30pm.

Customers are currently not allowed to enter the shop but can place orders from outside. We politely request that all customers wear face coverings in the vicinity of the shop and maintain a safe distance at all times.
